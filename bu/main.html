Experience

Sound, music and spoken words in immersive, interactive 3D worlds. The idea is simple: each moment of a song or podcast can be used as key in both time and 3D space. Soundcloud lets you add comments along a 2D timeline; ElNoise does that (and more) in 3D. Now you don't have to be a game developer to create and publish your own unique, personalized virtual world.

Create

Tweak a public clip or upload your own, which is encoded, labelled, indexed and made available for distribution with secure URLs. Using the browser, webplayer, mobile app or standalone, you can customize an immersive experience, sequenced to one or more audio clips: colors, 3D objects, text, pictures, even whole environments.

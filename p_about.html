ServiceMedia is an Austin-based publisher of immersive, realtime 3D applications like these:

  • Space/Time RailRoad - Travel the metaverse!

  • Eloquent Noise - Every song is a virtual world

  • Augmentia - Reality augmentation tool

  • Philosophers Garden - Symbolic landscaping

ServiceMedia is developing a cloud-based platform for creating and sharing these Augmented/Virtual reality experiences, supporting cross-platform publishing (ios, android, standalones, web), multi-input schemes(keyboard/mouse, touch, handheld controllers, head/gaze tracking, ?) deep-linking via shareable shortcodes, and user-generated content management.
